Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our diamonds and services

What are net-zero natural diamonds?

Net-zero natural diamonds are mined diamonds that are produced in independently audited, carbon-neutral facilities powered by renewable energy. They are fully traceable from mine to masterpiece, and are verifiably climate-neutral without offsets or greenwashing.

What's the difference between natural and lab-grown diamonds?

Both are real diamonds with identical physical, chemical, and optical properties. Natural diamonds are formed in the earth over billions of years, while lab-grown diamonds are created in controlled environments. Lab-grown typically cost up to 95% less than natural diamonds of equivalent quality.

How does the custom design process work?

We discuss your vision and budget during a consultation. Our designers create 3D renderings for your approval with unlimited revisions. Once approved, we craft your ring with expert precision. Most custom rings are completed within 4-6 weeks.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes! We offer flexible, interest-free payment plans to make your dream ring more affordable. We'll work with you to find a payment structure that fits your budget comfortably.

What's included in your lifetime warranty?

Our comprehensive lifetime warranty covers all manufacturing defects, including issues with the setting, prongs, and metalwork. We stand behind the quality of our craftsmanship.

Can I see diamonds in person before purchasing?

Absolutely! We encourage in-person showroom visits where you can see and compare diamonds side-by-side. We also offer virtual consultations with high-quality video for those who prefer to shop remotely.
