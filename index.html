Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about lab-grown diamonds

What are lab-grown diamonds?

Lab-grown diamonds are real diamonds created in controlled laboratory environments using advanced technology. They have the same physical, chemical, and optical properties as mined diamonds. The only difference is their origin.

Are all lab-grown diamonds the same quality?

No. Just like mined diamonds, lab-grown diamonds vary significantly in quality. At House of Diamonds, we exclusively source premium-grade lab-grown diamonds with exceptional cut, color, and clarity.

How much can I save with a lab-grown diamond?

Lab-grown diamonds typically cost 40-70% less than equivalent mined diamonds. This means you can get a larger, higher-quality diamond for your budget, or invest those savings in a more elaborate setting.

How does the custom design process work?

We discuss your vision and budget during a consultation. Our designers create 3D renderings for your approval with unlimited revisions. Once approved, we craft your ring with expert precision.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes! We offer flexible, interest-free payment plans to make your dream ring more affordable. We'll work with you to find a payment structure that fits your budget comfortably.

What's included in your lifetime warranty?

Our comprehensive lifetime warranty covers all manufacturing defects, including issues with the setting, prongs, and metalwork. We stand behind the quality of our craftsmanship.

How long does it take to create a custom ring?

Most custom rings are completed within 4-6 weeks. This includes design consultation, 3D rendering approval, and expert crafting. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your consultation.
